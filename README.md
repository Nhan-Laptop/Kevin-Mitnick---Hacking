Kevin Mitnick is a well-known hacker who gained notoriety in the 1990s for his hacking activities. He was involved in various cybercrimes, including unauthorized access to computer systems, stealing sensitive information, and evading law enforcement. Mitnick's hacking techniques included social engineering, where he manipulated individuals to gain access to systems, and exploiting vulnerabilities in software and networks. He was eventually arrested in 1995 and served time in prison for his crimes. After his release, Mitnick became a security consultant and author, sharing his knowledge and experiences to help organizations improve their cybersecurity defenses.
